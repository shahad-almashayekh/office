about us

The office of Eng. Shukri Al-Mashaikh is an engineering consultancy office that provides consulting, design and engineering supervision services since 1972, as it was founded by the architect Shukri Al-Mashaikh in Amman and was one of the first engineering offices in the Kingdom. It is appropriate to the surrounding environment, taking into account the developments that take place in the field of architecture and design to keep its customers always up to date with everything new in the field of architecture We also cooperate with the best in all fields of engineering from electrical engineering, mechanical engineering and structural engineering in order for our client to obtain integrated plans that conform to the Jordanian and international specifications and standards according to the approved codes, which leads the customer to save effort and money during the implementation process as the plans do not need any modification from the contractors Implementers of the project Finally, we hope that we will meet your expectations and look forward to cooperating with you in all your construction projects inside and outside the Hashemite Kingdom of Jordan.

Our vision

Taking into account architectural specifications and standards in all engineering designs, taking into account the need to provide an aesthetic and functional aspect in all projects And to provide integrated engineering projects (design, implementation, supervision) that keep pace with the various developments in the engineering and architectural fields and are in harmony with the desires and requirements of different customers and in the best possible way.
